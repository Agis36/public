## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 💳 Subscriptions & Payments

#### How do I cancel or modify my monthly/annual subscription?
All in-app purchases and subscriptions are handled safely and securely directly by Apple. Our team does not have access to bill you or cancel your Apple ID subscription directly. To manage your subscriptions, please follow these steps on your iOS device:
1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone or iPad.
2. Tap your **Name / Apple ID** at the very top of the screen.
3. Tap **Subscriptions**.
4. Tap **MathQuest AI** from your active subscription list.
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription** (or choose a different tier).

#### Can I share my single subscription with other devices?
Yes! As long as your devices are logged into the same Apple ID, or if you have **Apple Family Sharing** enabled, you can utilize your subscription across multiple family devices. To restore your purchase on a secondary device, tap the **Restore Purchases** or **Premium** button on the home screen.

### 🎙️ Microphones, Permissions & On-Device Speech

#### Why is the microphone voice answering feature not recognizing my child's answer?
MathQuest AI performs core speech recognition safely on-device to ensure strict child privacy compliance. If speech-to-text is not operating correctly, please ensure microphone permissions are granted:
1. Open your iOS **Settings** app.
2. Scroll down and locate **MathQuest AI** in your apps list.
3. Ensure the toggle switch next to **Microphone** is turned **ON** green.
4. Ensure your child speaks clearly after tapping the microphone button, and awaits the text recognition indicator.

---

### 💾 Profile Setup & Data

#### Can I create separate profiles for multiple children on one device?
Absolutely! MathQuest AI supports up to **3 customer-configurable childhood profiles** concurrently on a single device slot.
1. Click the **Profile** selector icon on the home screen.
2. Tap **Add New** or any editable avatar coin.
3. Choose their learning stage (Easy: 4-7 years, Medium: 7-10 years, Hard: 10-13 years) to automatically customize the curriculum.
4. Individual score streaks and achievements will be saved locally on-device for each profile.

#### Is my child's math camera crop history uploaded to your servers?
No. Image processing from the **Math Scanner camera utility** is filtered and translated locally. We do not store, retain, or share photos of handwritten equations or kids on external cloud databases.
